OUR MISSION

Unlocking the world’s potential through problem solving

What We Do

Our team consists of European Girls’ Mathematical Olympiad and European Girls’ Olympiad in Informatics participants who have firsthand experience in the challenges of preparing for international competitions. We provide curated problem sets, structured courses, and interactive learning resources that cover every aspect of competitive mathematics and science. Prism also connects learners with peers worldwide, creating a community where students can study together, exchange ideas, and motivate each other.

Our Approach

We believe in learning through interactive problem-solving rather than passive memorization. Courses are designed to guide students step by step, offering hints, challenges, and real-world applications to develop deep understanding. Students can progress at their own pace, skip ahead if ready, and receive immediate feedback through AI-assisted grading and hints.

Our Learners

Prism welcomes students from all countries and backgrounds who are interested in mathematics, physics, and other STEM competitions. Whether beginners or advanced competitors, anyone motivated to improve, explore, and challenge themselves can benefit.

Our Courses

Our curriculum covers the core fields of mathematical olympiads including combinatorics, algebra, number theory, and geometry. Each course is designed to develop deep problem-solving ability through carefully selected problems and guided learning.
